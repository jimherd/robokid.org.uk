To provide a more flexible system we now supply the video material through two ways

 

1.    Through the supplied DVD

2.    Through the web site YouTube (www.youtube.com)

 

We had planed to include TeacherTube as it has a remit to only include education based materials rather than the broader YouTube.  Some local authorities block YouTube from schools networks.  However, we have been unable, as yet, to upload the videos to their web site.  

 

In the longer term we would like to provide the video material through internet sites such as TeacherTube and YouTube.  However, at the moment, it seems that not all classroom facilities can cope so we have reverted to supplying a DVD disk.  

.

Before running the project, please check that you can display the video on the equipment available to you and we will be happy to help you with and issues that arise.
